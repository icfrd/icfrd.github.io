带消防通道的无障碍电梯系统 | Barrier Free Elevator System with Fire Access

2021-11-16
Technology

专利名称 / PATENT NAME: 带消防通道的无障碍电梯系统 / BARRIER FREE ELEVATOR SYSTEM WITH FIRE ACCESS

简介 / BRIEF

  • 预制电梯底坑与消防通道一体成型，电梯井道更稳固，最大程度避免电梯竖井倾斜、下沉。

The prefabricated elevator pit is integrated with the fire passage to make the elevator shaft more stable and avoid the inclination and sinking of the elevator shaft to the greatest extent.

  • 电梯直接通过柔性连接半层平台，不用连廊，不加建入户平台，不打破楼体建筑原有平衡。

The elevator is directly connected to the half floor platform through flexibility, without corridor, without adding entrance platform, and without breaking the original balance of the building.

  • 按需安装楼层间升降平台，成本更优化。

The lifting platform between floors is installed on demand, and the cost is more optimized.

  • 多个连续的楼层同时安装升降平台，可以形成垂直电梯的备用无障碍升降平台；
  • 垂直电梯检修楼层在二层或以上，检修时一层电梯井加装底板，形成无障碍通行通道。
