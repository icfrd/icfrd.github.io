专利名称 / PATENT NAME: 人性化无延迟涡轮增压发动机 / HUMANIZED TURBOCHARGED ENGINE WITHOUT DELAY

简介 / BRIEF

抬起油门时，切断发动机给涡轮增压的供气，避免陷入涡轮增压2秒延迟的死亡陷阱！

When lifting the throttle, cut off the air supply from the engine to the turbocharger to avoid falling into the death trap of 2-second delay by turbocharger!

传统涡轮增压发动机带来的恶性循环和死亡陷阱：抬起油门时，发动机废气仍推动涡轮增压工作，并持续2秒，你会感觉到汽车还在加速！错误的潜意识心理暗示会让你以为自己刚才松开的是刹车，想踩刹车的条件反射会促使你又错误地踩向了油门..

（下图1，为“无延迟涡轮增压发动机”踩下油门时的工作原理）

The vicious circle and death trap brought by the traditional turbocharged engine: when you lift the accelerator, the engine exhaust still drives the turbocharged work for 2 seconds, and you will feel that the car is still accelerating! The wrong subconscious psychological hint will make you think you just released the brake, then the conditioned reflex of wanting to step on the brake will make you step on the accelerator again by mistake..
