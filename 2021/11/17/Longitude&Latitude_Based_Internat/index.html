The Internat system that uses latitude & longitude as network addressing. The first URL example of Mount Qomolangma should be: http://86.9,27.9,8848,1

  • 真实的地理地址，使世界变得所见即所得。

The real geographical address makes the world what you see is what you get.

No core router is needed to realize real freedom; Based on precision control, management is closer to grass-roots democracy.

  • 以地理最近为路由基本原则，不同ISP实现自动直连；没有网络瓶颈。

Taking geographical proximity as the fundamental principles of routing protocol, then different ISPs realize automatic direct connection, without network bottlenecks.

  • 经纬地址天生即拥有，人人生来平等。

Longitude and latitude address is innate, so that every user is born with equal.

  • 可以直接实景建模，所得即所用，不受外界干扰。

It can be modeled directly, so what you get is what you want without external interference.

  • 路由协议简单普适，几乎无管理成本。

The routing protocol is simple and universal, without management cost almost.

Can coexist with TCP/IP network.
