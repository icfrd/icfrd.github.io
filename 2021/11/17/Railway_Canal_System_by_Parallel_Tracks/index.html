专利名称 / PATENT NAME: 基于并行轨道的铁路运河系统 / RAILWAY CANAL SYSTEM BY PARALLEL TRACKS

简介 / BRIEF

运行在多条并行的铁轨上的列车，通过一致协调的行动，将（集装箱）轮船通过陆地，从一个大洋运往另一个大洋彼岸。

Trains running on multiple parallel tracks can transport (container) ships from one ocean to the other ocean through concerted action.
