项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

新疆-瓦罕-瓜达尔 | XinJiang - Wakhan - Gwadar Line

负压气垫超级高铁系统全长约3653公里，其中中国境内约1379公里。

The total length of Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop is about 3653 km, including about 1379 km pipeline in China.

新疆-卡拉奇 | XinJiang - Karachi Line

长约2399公里，其中中国境内约252公里。

The total length of Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop is about 2399 km, including about 252 km pipeline in China.

说明：本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

Footnote: This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Power Generation System System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.
