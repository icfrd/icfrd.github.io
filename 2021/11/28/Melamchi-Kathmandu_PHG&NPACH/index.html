尼泊尔，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Nepal, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

超级高铁起点拟位于马兰奇，终点在尼泊尔首都加德满都，长约41公里，输水发电管全长约67公里。（含跨越山峰增加的8公里）

The Hyperloop will start from Melamchi and end at Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, with length of about 41 km, and the total length of pipeline for hydroelectric generation is about 67km. (including added 8 kilometers of mountain crossing)

输水发电管取水口拟位于马兰奇主供水点和印德拉瓦提河上游，海拔高程约1900米处。

The water intake of Pipeline Power Generation System is drafted to be located at upstream of the Melamchi Water Supply Head Poiny and the Indrawati River, with altitude of about 1900 meters.

马兰奇以下5公里的印德拉瓦提河段，为与马兰奇-皮姆讷格尔 输水发电管及超级高铁共用的部分。

There are 5km Indrawati River segment downstream of Melamchi is shared with the Melamchi - Bhimnagar PPGS & NPACH.

从印德拉瓦提河到加德满都，有16公里的山峦需要翻越，最高峰海拔高程为1756米。

From Indrawati River to Kathmandu, there are 16 kilometers of mountains to climb, and the altitude of highest peak is 1756 meters .

在加德满都境内，管线从1400米海拔高度，沿着曼纳哈拉河方向顺流而下。

In Kathmandu, the pipeline flows down the Manohara River from altitude of 1400 meters.

管线末端位于海拔高度为1293米的哈奴玛蒂河和曼纳哈拉河的交汇处，毗邻特里布万国际机场。

The end of the pipeline is located at the intersection of the Hanumante River and Manohara River with altitude of 1293 meters, adjacent to the Tribhuvan International Airport.

说明：本项目是马兰奇-皮姆讷格尔 输水发电管及超级高铁的姊妹项目，采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

Footnote: This project is the sister project of Melamchi - Bhimnagar PPGS & NPACH, utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Power Generation System System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.
