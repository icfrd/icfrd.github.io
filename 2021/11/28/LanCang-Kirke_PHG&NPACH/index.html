of Myanmar, Laos, Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, Papua New Guinea and Australia Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

输水发电管线将始于澜沧江下游，途径缅甸、老挝、泰国、柬埔寨，到达湄公河越南出海口，沿海岸线经泰国、马来西亚、新加坡、印度尼西亚、巴布新几内亚，其中将修建桥港一体的跨海大桥和隧道，最终到达澳大利亚喀尔克河出海口内湖，全长11186公里以上。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

The hydroelectric generation pipeline will start from the lower reaches of LanCang River, pass through Myanmar, Laos, Thailand and Cambodia, reach the Sea outlet of Mekong River in Vietnam, then pass through Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia and Papua New Guinea along the coastline, in whichbridge and port integrated sea crossing tunnels will be built, and finally reach the inner lake at the Sea outlet of Kirke River in Australia, the total length is more than 11186 kilometers.

输水发电管取水口拟位于中国云南西双版纳附近的澜沧江上，海拔高度约540米，管线沿澜沧江和湄公河顺流而下。

The water intake of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation is drafted to be located on the LanCang River near XiShuangBanNa, Yunnan, China, with altitude of about 540 meters, and the pipeline flows downstream along the LanCang River and Mekong River.
