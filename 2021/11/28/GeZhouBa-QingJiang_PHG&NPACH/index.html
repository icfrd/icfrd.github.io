中国宜昌，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Yichang China, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目是恩施-宜昌 输水发电管及超级高铁的姊妹项目，采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术。

输水发电管取水口拟位于葛洲坝水电站的上游侧，黄柏河支流与长江的交汇处，海拔高程约60米，倚靠沿江大道顺流而下，全长约49公里。

This project is the sister project of EnShi - YiChang PHG & NPACH, utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc.

The water intake of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation is drafted to be located at the upstream of GeZhouBa Hydropower Station, the intersection of the Yangtze River and HuangBaiHe River which its tributary, with altitude of about 66 meters, downstream along the YanJiang Avenue, the total length is about 49 kilometers.

负压气垫超级高铁起点拟位于石子岭路与沿江大道交汇处。

The starting point of the Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop is drafted to be located at the intersection of ShiZiLing Road and YanJiang Avenue.

管线末端位于枝江善溪冲水库长江口，海拔高程约39米；超级高速终点位于曹岗路与松岗路交汇处，与恩施-宜昌 输水发电管及超级高铁相联，对岸就是清江入长江口，毗陵318国道和紫云铁路枝江沙湾段。

The end of the pipeline is at the estuary of ShanXiChong Reservoir of ZhiJiang District nearby the Yangtze River, with altitude of about 39 meters; The ending point of the Hyperloop is located at the intersection of CaoGang Road and SongGang Road, connected to the EnShi - YiChang PHG & NPACH, and its opposite bank is the estuary of Qingjiang River to Yangtze River, adjoin the National Highway 318 and ZhiJiang ShaWan section of ZiYun railway.

另一个更可行的上游取水方案：在黄柏河与长江交汇口上游24公里处的寨其沟水库取水，超级高铁的起点也可以相应地向上游延伸；虽然拉长了管线工程长度，但也会给宜昌市夷陵区带来交通便利。

Another more feasible upstream water intake scheme: take water from ZhaiQiGou Reservoir 24 kilometers upstream of the intersection of HuangBaiHe River and the Yangtze Riverr, so starting point of the Hyperloop can also extend upstream accordingly; Although the length of the pipeline project is lengthened, it will also bring convenient transportation to YiLing District of Yichang City.

寨其沟水库海拔高度116米，此处取水对葛洲坝不会产生直接影响。

The altitude of ZhaiQiGou Reservoir is 116 meters, and the water intake here will not have direct impact on Gezhouba Dam.

输水发电管线沿着长江左岸边坡越过葛洲坝，穿过葛洲坝公园后至沿江大道。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline crosses GeZhouBa Dam along the left bank slope of the Yangtze River, passes through GeZhouBa Park and then reaches YanJiang Avenue.
