中国湖北宜昌、恩施，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Yichang and EnShi, HuBei, China, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

葛洲坝-清江 主线 | GeZhouBa - QingJiang Main Line

输水发电管取水口拟位于宜昌市夷陵区黄柏河上的寨其沟水库，管线沿着黄柏河和长江边的沿江大道顺流而下，末端位于宜昌市枝江善溪冲水库长江口，全长约71公里。

The water intake of Pipeline Power Generation System is drafted to be located in ZhaiQiGou Reservoir on the HuangBaiHe River in YiLing District of Yichang City. The pipeline flows downstream along the HuangBaiHe River and YanJiang Avenue beside the Yangtze River, and the end is located at the estuary of ShanXiChong Reservoir in ZhiJiang District of YiChang City nearby the Yangtze River, with total length of about 71 kilometers.

寨其沟水库海拔高度116米，此处取水对葛洲坝水电站不会产生直接影响。

The altitude of ZhaiQiGou Reservoir is 116 meters, and the water intake here will not have direct impact on GeZhouBa Hydropower Station.

输水发电管线沿着长江左岸边坡越过葛洲坝，穿过葛洲坝公园后至沿江大道。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline crosses GeZhouBa Dam along the left bank slope of the Yangtze River, passes through GeZhouBa Park and then reaches YanJiang Avenue.

管线末端的长江海拔高程约36米。超级高速终点位于曹岗路与松岗路交汇处，毗陵318国道和紫云铁路枝江沙湾段，与 恩施-宜昌支线 相联，对岸就是清江入长江口。

The altitude at the end of the pipeline is about 36 meters. The Hyperloop terminal is located at the intersection of CaoGang Road and SongGang Road, adjoin the National Highway 318 and ZhiJiang ShaWan section of ZiYun Railway, then connected to the EnShi - YiChang Branch and its opposite bank is the estuary of Qingjiang River to Yangtze River.

上游延展线 | Upstream Extension Line

输水发电管线向寨其沟水库的上游延展，延展线长约28公里。

The pipeline extends to the upstream of ZhaiQiGou Reservoir, with length of about 28 km.

取水口拟在尚家河水库上游，海拔高度约240米；

The water intake is planned to be at the upstream of ShangJiaHe Reservoir, with altitude of about 240 meters;

兴山-宜昌支线 | XingShan - YiChang Branch

输水发电管线拟始于宜昌兴山古洞口水库，沿着长江顺流而下，途径昭君镇和秭归，终点拟与 葛洲坝-清江主线 相连，全长约123公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from GuDongKou Reservoir in XingShan of YiChang China, and will flow down the Yangtze River, pass through ZhaoJun town and ZiGui, and the end point is planned to be connected with GeZhouBa - QingJiang Main Line, with total length of more than 123 km.

取水口拟在古洞口水电站的上游，海拔高度约315米；

The water intake is planned to be located at the upstream of GuDongKou Hydropower Station, with altitude of about 315 meters.

附注：本支线需尽可能（但不必须）优先兑现若干小股东的有形资产坏账。

Note: The investors should give priority to cashing the tangible asset based bad debts of several minority shareholders as much as possible (but not necessarily) for this Branch.

远安-当阳支线 | YuanAn - DangYang Branch

输水发电管线拟始于峡口水库，长约200公里。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from XiaKou Reservoir with length of about 200 km.

取水口海拔高度约220米。

The altitude of the water intake is about 220 meters.

恩施-宜昌支线 | EnShi - YiChang Branch

起点拟位于湖北恩施市，终点在湖北宜昌市枝江，沿清江顺流而下，全长约255公里，在宜万铁路恩施段的南部。

It will start from EnShi of HuBei Province and end at ZhiJiang District of Yichang City, HuBei Province. It flows down the QingJiang River, with total length of about 255 km, at the south of EnShi section of YiChang-WanZhou Railway.

输水发电管取水口拟位于清江姚家坪水电站的上游侧，海拔高程约630米。

The water intake of Pipeline Power Generation System is drafted to be located at the upstream of YaoJiaPing Hydropower Station of QingJiang River, with altitude of about 630 meters.

姚家坪水电站位于恩施市东北方向38公里处，清江流经恩施市区。

YaoJiaPing Hydropower Station is located 38 kilometers northeast of EnShi City, and the QingJiang River flows through the Enshi City.

管线拟穿过清江水布垭水电站的山体。（ 部分管线，比如观光支线，可能位于水底，与宜万铁路类似？）

The pipeline is drafted to pass through the mountains of QingJiang ShuiBuYa Hydropower Station. ( Some parts of the pipeline, such as sightseeing branch line may be underwater, similar to YiChang-WanZhou Railway? )

直接从隔河岩水库到达高坝洲下游，而不绕经隔河岩水电站和高坝洲水电站之间的流域。

It runs directly from GehHeYan Reservoir to the lower reaches of GaoBaZhou without bypassing the watershed between GehHeYan Hydropower Station and GaoBaZhou Hydropower Station.

末端跨越长江，与 葛洲坝-清江主线 相连。

The end crosses the Yangtze River and is connected with the GeZhouBa - QingJiang Main Line.

说明：本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制、带大小写和复数属性的中文格式。
