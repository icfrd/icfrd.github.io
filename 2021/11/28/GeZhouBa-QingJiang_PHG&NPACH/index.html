中国宜昌，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Yichang China, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目是恩施-宜昌 输水发电管及超级高铁的姊妹项目，采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

输水发电管取水口拟位于宜昌市夷陵区黄柏河上的寨其沟水库，管线沿着黄柏河和长江边的沿江大道顺流而下，末端位于宜昌市枝江善溪冲水库长江口，全长约71公里。

This project is the sister project of EnShi - YiChang PHG & NPACH, utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

The water intake of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation is drafted to be located in ZhaiQiGou Reservoir on the HuangBaiHe River in YiLing District of Yichang City. The pipeline flows downstream along the HuangBaiHe River and YanJiang Avenue beside the Yangtze River, and the end is located at the estuary of ShanXiChong Reservoir in ZhiJiang District of YiChang City nearby the Yangtze River, with total length of about 71 kilometers.

寨其沟水库海拔高度116米，此处取水对葛洲坝水电站不会产生直接影响。

The altitude of ZhaiQiGou Reservoir is 116 meters, and the water intake here will not have direct impact on GeZhouBa Hydropower Station.

输水发电管线沿着长江左岸边坡越过葛洲坝，穿过葛洲坝公园后至沿江大道。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline crosses GeZhouBa Dam along the left bank slope of the Yangtze River, passes through GeZhouBa Park and then reaches YanJiang Avenue.

管线末端的长江海拔高程约36米。超级高速终点位于曹岗路与松岗路交汇处，毗陵318国道和紫云铁路枝江沙湾段，与恩施-宜昌 输水发电管及超级高铁相联，对岸就是清江入长江口。

The altitude at the end of the pipeline is about 36 meters. The Hyperloop terminal is located at the intersection of CaoGang Road and SongGang Road, adjoin the National Highway 318 and ZhiJiang ShaWan section of ZiYun Railway, then connected to the EnShi - YiChang PHG & NPACH and its opposite bank is the estuary of Qingjiang River to Yangtze River.
