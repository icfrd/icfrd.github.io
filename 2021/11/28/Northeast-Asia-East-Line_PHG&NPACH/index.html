东北亚东线，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

East Line of Northeast Asia, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

含图们江线和珲春支线，全长约300公里以上；

Including Tumen River Line and HunChun Branch Line, with total length of more than 300km;

图们江线 | Tumen River Line

图们江线输水发电管线拟始于中国吉林延边海兰水库，到达图们江顺流而下，直至朝鲜罗先市最东端的港口，长约222公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from HaiLan Reservoir in YanBian of JiLin China, flow down the Tumen River and reach the easternmost port of Raseon, North Korea, with total length of more than 222 km;

图们江线取水口拟在海兰水电站的上游，海拔高度约165米；

The water intake of Tumen River Line is planned to be at the upstream of HaiLan hydropower station, with altitude of about 165 meters;

管线经中朝边界、俄朝边界，在图们江出海口向西南到达终点港口；后期工程再沿海岸线向对马海峡方向修建。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline runs along the border between China and North Korea and the border between Russia and North Korea, and reaches the terminal port in the southwest at the outlet of Tumen River; In the later stage, the project will be built along the coastline to the Tsushima Strait.

珲春支线 | HunChun Branch Line

珲春支线从图们江经中国吉林珲春市，直至俄罗斯滨海边疆区海岸，沿海岸线向南与图们江线汇合，长约78公里以上。

The HunChun Branch Line runs from the Tumen River through HunChun City of JiLing China to the coast of the Primorye-Territory of Russian, and joins the Tumen River Line to the south along the coastline, with length of more than 78 km.
