Projects

中国、缅甸、泰国、孟加拉、印度、巴基斯坦、伊朗、阿联酋、阿曼、也门、吉布提、沙特和埃及等，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of China, Myanmar, Thailand, Bangladesh, India, Pakistan, Iran, UAE, Oman, Yemen, Djibouti, Saudi Arabia and Egypt etc, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术。

输水发电管线将始于怒江中国云南段，终点位于尼罗河埃及段，含南亚-尼罗河段和怒江段，全长约13960公里；其中，南亚-尼罗河段沿南亚大陆板块海岸线向西，长约12635公里。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc.

The hydroelectric generation pipeline will start from the NuJiang River in YunNan of China and end at the Nile River in Egypt, including the South Asia-Nile Section and the NuJiang River Section, with total length of about 13960 km; the South Asia-Nile Section runs westward along the coastline of the South Asia continental plate, with length of about 12635 km.

输水发电管起点拟于中缅边境上游4.7公里处的怒江上；江中天然分水岭的上游侧保留天然水道，尽量减小对周边环境的影响，取水口位于分水岭下游侧，海拔高度约570米，管线沿怒江顺流而下；

The starting point of the Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation is drafted to be on the NuJiang River 4.7 kilometers upstream of the China-Myanmar border; It is plan to retain the natural waterway on the upstream side of the natural watershed in the river to minimize the impact on the surrounding environment. The water intake is located on the downstream side of the watershed, with altitude of about 570 meters. The pipeline flows down the NuJiang River;

管线怒江段途径缅甸与泰国边境，缅泰共有长度约为231公里，怒江段总长约1325公里；

The NuJiang River Section of the pipeline will pass through Myanmar and the Myanmar-Thailand border, the shared pipeline length on the Myanmar-Thailand border is about 231km, and the total length of NuJiang River Section is about 1325km;

管线怒江段末端位于怒江出海口；

The pipeline end of NuJiang River Section is located at the estuary of NuJiang River;

南亚-尼罗河管线与怒江段相接后，横跨霍尔木兹海峡的长度约57公里，拟定修建桥港一体的跨海大桥和隧道；

After the South Asia-Nile pipeline is connected with the NuJiang River Section, it crosses the Hormuz Strait for about 57 kilometers. It is planned to build a sea crossing bridge and port integrated tunnel;

南亚-尼罗河管线途径迪拜和阿布扎比附近区域后，向南到达靠近亚丁湾的低海拔海岸沿线；

After the South Asia-Nile pipeline passes through the areas near Dubai and Abu Dhabi, it goes south to the low-altitude coast near the Gulf of Aden;

南亚-尼罗河管线绕过曼德海峡，沿红海东岸线低海拔线路再向北；

The South Asia-Nile pipeline bypasses the Bab al-Mandab Strait, and then goes north along the low-altitude line on the east coast of the Red Sea;

在曼德海峡上修建一座桥港一体的跨海大桥和隧道，连通到红海西岸贝雷索的支线，支线长度约228公里，其中跨海大桥长约30公里；

It will build a sea crossing bridge and port integrated tunnel on Bab al-Mandab Strait, to connect to Beraisole at the end of the branch line on the West Bank of the Red Sea. The length of the branch line is about 228km, of which the sea crossing bridge is about 30km long;

红海东岸的南亚-尼罗河管线将跨过亚喀巴湾和苏伊士湾，沿苏伊士湾西岸低海拔线路向北；

The South Asia-Nile pipeline on the East Bank of the Red Sea will cross Aqaba Gulf and Khalij as-Suways, and go north along the low-altitude line on the West Bank of Khalij as-Suways;

南亚-尼罗河管线通过蒂朗岛跨越亚喀巴湾的大桥长约7.2公里和5.8公里，拟定修建桥港一体的跨海大桥和隧道；

The bridges of South Asia-Nile pipeline crossing Aqaba Gulf through Tiran island is about 7.2km and 5.8km long. It is drafted to build a sea crossing bridge and port integrated tunnel;

南亚-尼罗河管线跨越苏伊士湾的大桥主桥长约4.8公里和14.5公里，拟定修建桥港一体的跨海大桥和隧道；

The main bridges of the South Asia-Nile pipeline crossing the Khalij as-Suways is about 4.8km and 14.5km long. It is planned to build a sea crossing bridge and port integrated tunnel;

沿苏伊士湾西岸的南亚-尼罗河管线到达苏伊士后，向西绕到新开罗和开罗上游地区（以便市政水网利用末端管道的水势能），最终汇入尼罗河。

After arriving in Suez along the South Asia-Nile pipeline on the West Bank of the Khalij as-Suways, it winds westward to the upper reaches of New Cairo and Cairo ( It is convenient for the Municipal Pipe Network to use the water potential energy of the end pipeline ) and finally flows into the Nile.
