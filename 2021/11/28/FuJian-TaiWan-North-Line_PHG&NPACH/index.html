中国福建、台湾，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁北线项目

FuJian and TaiWan of China, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop North Line Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

南路方案 | South Road Plan

输水发电管线拟始于福建闽江上游水口水库，沿着闽江顺流而下，通过平潭-新竹新修的跨海大桥到台湾，终点拟位于台湾屏东龙銮潭，全长约680公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from the ShuiKou Reservoir in the upper reaches of the MinJiang River in FuJian of China, flow down the MinJiang River, and reach TaiWan of China through the newly built sea crossing bridge between PingTan and Hsinchu. The terminal point is planned to be located in LongLuanTan Reservoir in TaiWan Pingtung , with total length of more than 680km.

取水口拟在水口水电站的上游，泄水底孔附近，海拔高度约60米；

The water intake is planned to be located at the upstream of ShuiKou Hydropower Station and near the bottom outlet, with altitude of about 60 meters;

管线到新竹后沿海岸线一路向南；

After the pipeline reaches Hsinchu, it goes all the way south along the coastline;

来自福建的淡水最终汇入屏东龙銮潭（台湾最南端的水库），海拔高度约15米。

Fresh water from FuJian eventually flows into LongLuanTan of Pingdong (the southernmost reservoir in TaiWan), with altitude of about 15 meters.

北路方案 | North Road Plan

输水发电管线拟始于福建闽江上游池坑水库，沿着闽江顺流而下，通过平潭-新竹新修的跨海大桥到台湾，终点拟位于台湾基隆翠湖（省中湖），全长约575公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from ChiKeng Reservoir in the upper reaches of MinJiang River in FuJian, flow down the Minjiang River, and reach Taiwan of China through the newly built sea crossing bridge between PingTan and Hsinchu. The terminal point is planned to be located in Keelung (Centrally) CuiHu Lake in TaiWan, with total length of more than 575km.

取水口拟在池坑水电站的上游，泄水底孔附近，海拔高度约123米；

The water intake is planned to be located at the upstream of ChiKeng Hydropower Station and near the bottom outlet, with altitude of about 123m;

管线沿着金溪顺流而下，途径富屯溪、闽江，到达福建平潭，经过新修建的台海通道，到达台湾新竹；

The hydroelectric generation pipeline flows downstream along JinXi River, passes through FuTunXi River and MinJiang River, reaches PingTan of FuJian, and reaches Hsinchu of TaiWan through the newly built TaiWan Strait Channel;

管线沿凤仙溪溯流而上，再向东北方向转接大汉溪，再经淡水河到基隆河，最终到达基隆翠湖，海拔高度约42米。

The pipeline flows upstream along FengXianXi River, then transfers to Dahan River in the northeast, then passes through Tamsui River to Keelung River, and finally reaches Keelung CuiHu Lake, with altitude of about 42 meters.

说明：本项目是恩施-宜昌 输水发电管及超级高铁的姊妹项目，采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制、带大小写和复数属性的中文格式。
