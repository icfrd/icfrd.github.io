加拿大、美国、墨西哥，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Canada, USA and Mexico, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Power Generation System System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

温哥华 - 诺福克 | Vancouver - Norfolk

温哥华 - 诺福克负压气垫超级高铁起点拟于加拿大温哥华市中心东端港口，沿现有公路进入美国境内，翻过落基山脉后，沿密苏里河流向东南方向，途径密歇根湖和伊利湖附近，以及华盛顿哥伦比亚特区，最终到达弗吉尼亚诺福克国际港口，超级高铁全长约5324公里（ 参考路线图：https://goo.gl/maps/WSBgNT8nQdbXGmiA6 ）。

The Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop of Vancouver - Norfolk will start from the port at the Downtown Eastside of Vancouver Canada, enter the United States along the existing highway, go southeast along the Missouri River after crossing the Rocky Mountains, and pass nearby regions of the Lake Michigan and Lake Erie along the Missouri River, and Washington, D.C., then finally reach the Norfolk International Port of Virginia. The total length of the Hyperloop is about 5324 kilometers ( Roadmap for reference: https://goo.gl/maps/WSBgNT8nQdbXGmiA6 ).

负压气垫超级高铁的喷气动力由输水发电管系统产生的清洁能源供应，输水发电管沿途补充水能并发电。
比如代阿布洛湖输水发电管及超级高铁支线，由代阿布洛湖风景区到比目鱼湾游艇俱乐部，全长140公里（ 参考路线图：https://goo.gl/maps/adkQUREZZVkinPEQ7 ）。它将为气动超级高铁翻山越岭提供动能，其自身也是优美的旅游观光线路。

The jet power of the Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop is supplied by clean energy generated by the Pipeline Power Generation System System, the hydroelectric generation pipe replenishes water power and generates electricity along the way.
For example, the Pipeline Power Generation System of Daiblo Lake and the Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop branch line are 140 kilometers ( Roadmap for reference: https://goo.gl/maps/adkQUREZZVkinPEQ7 ) from Daiblo Lake scenic spot to the Flounder Bay Yacht Club. It will provide kinetic energy for the Air Hyperloop to cross mountains, and it is also graceful tourist and sightseeing itinerary in itself.

代阿布洛湖支线的取水口拟设在拱坝下游，不会直接对水坝产生影响，取水口到管线的距离约1.5公里。

The water intake of Daiblo Lake branch is drafted to be set at the downstream of the arch dam, which will not directly affect the dam. The distance from the water intake to the pipeline is about 1.5km.

温哥华-拉古纳萨拉达 | Vancouver - Laguna Salada

温哥华-拉古纳萨拉达负压气垫超级高铁起点拟于加拿大温哥华市中心东端港口，沿海岸线现有公路向南进入美国境内，最终到达墨西哥拉古纳萨拉达湖，超级高铁全长约2445公里（ 参考路线图：https://goo.gl/maps/ZCvKY4ttgw7bSwgi8 ）。
PS：在美国境内可不设经停站点，只提供动力和服务。

The Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop of Vancouver - Laguna Salada will start from the port at the Downtown Eastside of Vancouver Canada, enters the United States southward along the existing highway along the coastline, and finally reaches Laguna Salada Lake of Mexico. The total length of the Hyperloop is about 2445km ( Roadmap for reference: https://goo.gl/maps/ZCvKY4ttgw7bSwgi8 )
PS: In the United States, only power and services can be provided without stops.
