埃及，基于并行轨道的铁路运河项目

of Egypt, Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用基于并行轨道的铁路运河系统等专利技术。

轨道线路沿苏伊士运河西奈河岸，以取得较为平坦的道路，减少施工量，个别地点地势起伏略高，平均海拔高度8米，全长约155公里。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks System etc.

The tracks line will along the Sinai bank of Suez Canal to obtain a relatively flat road and reduce the construction works. The terrain of individual sites is slightly higher, with average altitude of 8 meters and total length of about 155 km.

起点和终点均没有障碍物。

There are no obstacles at the beginning and end.

适合修建大型港口和集装箱货轮升降船坞。

It is suitable for building large ports and container ship lifting docks.
