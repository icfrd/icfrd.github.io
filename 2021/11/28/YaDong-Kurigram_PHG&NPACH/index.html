中国、不丹、印度、孟加拉，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of China, Bhutan, India and Bangladesh, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术。

超级高铁起点拟于中国西藏亚东县，途径不丹、印度，终点在孟加拉古里格拉姆县境内，输水发电管全长约244公里。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc.

The Hyperloop will start from Yadong County of Tibet China, pass through Bhutan and India, and end in Kurigram District of Bangladesh. The total length of water transmission and hydroelectric generation pipeline is about 244km.

拟在多加咔山下建设一个矮坝，作为输水发电管的取水口，海拔高程约2820米。

It is drafted to build a low dam at the foot of DuoJiaKa mountain as the water intake of water transmission and Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation, with an altitude of about 2820 meters.

多加咔坡地外观长约809米、宽357米，与河谷路线长约896米，拟采用基于风洞的助力系统专利技术，在坡地上的山体内建设一个风洞作为短起降跑道机场，机场与山谷中的交通线采用负压气垫超级高铁系统相联。

The appearance of DuoJiaKa slope is about 809m long and 357m wide, and the length of the valley route is about 896m. It is drafted to adopt the patented technology of Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System to build a wind tunnel in the mountain on the slope as a short takeoff and landing runway airport, which is connected with the traffic line in the valley through Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System.

管线途径中国西藏康布麻曲、不丹阿莫曲、印度托尔萨河，和孟加拉杜德库马河，末端位于与贾木纳河交汇处，海拔高度约为25米。

The pipeline will pass through KanBuMaQu Chhu in Tibet China, Amo Chhu in Bhutan, Torsa River in India and Dud Kumar River in Bangladesh. The end will locate at the intersection with Jamuna River, with altitude of about 25 meters.
