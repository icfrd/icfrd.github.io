2022高海拔水力发电与应用前景展望 | 2022' Application Prospect of High-Altitude Hydroelectricity

2022-02-18
Activities

日期 / DATE: 18/02/2022 - 20/02/2022

方式 / METHOD: 线下和线上 / Offline & Online

地址 / LOCATION: 中国宜昌及全球 / Yichang of China & ALL

背景 / BACKGROUND

当前水力发电站需要修筑大型的工程，不但只能对流经的水体进行一次性利用发电，而且会造成对鱼类回游和生态环境的影响。除了能削峰填谷、改善河道，水电站还能为促进人文交流和生态文明做什么贡献呢？

At present, hydroelectric power station needs large-scale construction, which can only be used once for electric power generation by the water flowing through, but will also have impacts on fish migration and ecological environment. In addition to peak shaving and valley filling, and improving river channels. What else can hydropower stations contribute to promoting people-to-people exchanges and ecological civilization?

葛洲坝-清江 输水发电管及超级高铁 | GeZhouBa - QingJiang PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国宜昌，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Yichang China, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目是恩施-宜昌 输水发电管及超级高铁的姊妹项目，采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

This project is the sister project of EnShi - YiChang PHG & NPACH, utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

输水发电管取水口拟位于宜昌市夷陵区黄柏河上的寨其沟水库，管线沿着黄柏河和长江边的沿江大道顺流而下，末端位于宜昌市枝江善溪冲水库长江口，全长约71公里。

The water intake of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation is drafted to be located in ZhaiQiGou Reservoir on the HuangBaiHe River in YiLing District of Yichang City. The pipeline flows downstream along the HuangBaiHe River and YanJiang Avenue beside the Yangtze River, and the end is located at the estuary of ShanXiChong Reservoir in ZhiJiang District of YiChang City nearby the Yangtze River, with total length of about 71 kilometers.

泰国-马来西亚铁路运河 | Thailand - Malaysia Railway Canal

2021-11-28
Projects

泰国、马来西亚，基于并行轨道的铁路运河项目

of Thailand and Malaysia, Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用基于并行轨道的铁路运河系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

轨道线路从泰国宋卡以南空旷海岸，避开机场等区域，向西南陆地直达对岸的马来西亚玻璃市以南空旷海岸，以取得较为平坦的道路，减少施工量，个别地点地势起伏略高，平均海拔高度49米，全长约100公里。

The track line will start from the spare coast south of Thailand Songkhla, keep away from the airport and other areas, and go to the southwest land to the spare coast south of Malaysia Negeri Perlis City, so as to obtain a relatively flat road and reduce the construction works. The terrain of individual sites fluctuates slightly, with average altitude of 49 meters and total length of about 100 km.
