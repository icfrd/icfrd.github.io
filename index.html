2022年高海拔水力发电的应用前景展望 | 2022' Application Prospect of High-Altitude Hydroelectricity

2022-02-18
Activities

日期 / DATE: 18/02/2022 - 20/02/2022

方式 / METHOD: 线下和线上 / Offline & Online

地址 / LOCATION: 中国宜昌及全球 / Yichang of China & ALL

背景 / BACKGROUND

当前水力发电站需要修筑大型的工程，不但只能对流经的水体进行一次性利用发电，而且会造成对鱼类回游和生态环境的影响。除了能削峰填谷、改善河道，水电站还能为促进交通做什么贡献呢？

At present, hydroelectric power station needs large-scale construction, which can only be used once for electric power generation by the water flowing through, but will also have impacts on fish migration and ecological environment. In addition to peak shaving and valley filling, and improving river channels, what contribution can hydropower stations make to promoting transportation?

葛洲坝-清江 输水发电管及超级高铁 | GeZhouBa - QingJiang PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国宜昌，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Yichang China, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目是恩施-宜昌 输水发电管及超级高铁的姊妹项目，采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术。

输水发电管取水口拟位于宜昌市夷陵区黄柏河上的寨其沟水库，管线沿着黄柏河和长江边的沿江大道顺流而下，末端位于宜昌市枝江善溪冲水库长江口，全长约71公里。

This project is the sister project of EnShi - YiChang PHG & NPACH, utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc.

The water intake of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation is drafted to be located in ZhaiQiGou Reservoir on the HuangBaiHe River in YiLing District of Yichang City. The pipeline flows downstream along the HuangBaiHe River and YanJiang Avenue beside the Yangtze River, and the end is located at the estuary of ShanXiChong Reservoir in ZhiJiang District of YiChang City nearby the Yangtze River, with total length of about 71 kilometers.

负压气垫超级高铁系统 | Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System

2021-11-21
Technology

专利名称 / PATENT NAME: 负压气垫超级高铁系统 / NEGATIVE PRESSURE AIR CUSHION HYPERLOOP SYSTEM

简介 / BRIEF

无需真空、无需磁悬浮、不耗电；通过喷射高压气体，在车前形成负压空间，车身四周形成气垫层，车后给予正压推力；可同时输送（增值）气体，具有施工成本低、结构简单、安全可靠、运行成本低、启停快，能转弯、上下坡等特点！

No need vacuum, no magnetic levitation, no electricity consumption; By spraying high-pressure gas, negative pressure space is formed in front of the vehicle, and air cushion layer is formed around the vehicle body, and positive pressure thrust is given behind the vehicle; It can transport (value-added) gas at the same time. It has the characteristics of low construction cost, simple structure, safety and reliability, low operation cost, fast start and stop, can turning, uphill and downhill, etc!
