2022年高海拔水力发电的应用前景展望 | 2022' Application Prospect of High-Altitude Hydroelectricity

2022-02-18
Activities

日期 / DATE: 18/02/2022 - 20/02/2022

方式 / METHOD: 线下和线上 / Offline & Online

地址 / LOCATION: 中国宜昌及全球 / Yichang of China & ALL

背景 / BACKGROUND

当前水力发电站需要修筑大型的工程，不但只能对流经的水体进行一次性利用发电，而且会造成对鱼类回游和生态环境的影响。除了能削峰填谷、改善河道，水电站还能为促进人文交流和生态文明做什么贡献呢？

At present, hydroelectric power station needs large-scale construction, which can only be used once for electric power generation by the water flowing through, but will also have impacts on fish migration and ecological environment. In addition to peak shaving and valley filling, and improving river channels. What else can hydropower stations contribute to promoting people-to-people exchanges and ecological civilization?

葛洲坝-清江 输水发电管及超级高铁 | GeZhouBa - QingJiang PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国宜昌，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Yichang China, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目是恩施-宜昌 输水发电管及超级高铁的姊妹项目，采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术。

输水发电管取水口拟位于宜昌市夷陵区黄柏河上的寨其沟水库，管线沿着黄柏河和长江边的沿江大道顺流而下，末端位于宜昌市枝江善溪冲水库长江口，全长约71公里。

This project is the sister project of EnShi - YiChang PHG & NPACH, utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc.

The water intake of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation is drafted to be located in ZhaiQiGou Reservoir on the HuangBaiHe River in YiLing District of Yichang City. The pipeline flows downstream along the HuangBaiHe River and YanJiang Avenue beside the Yangtze River, and the end is located at the estuary of ShanXiChong Reservoir in ZhiJiang District of YiChang City nearby the Yangtze River, with total length of about 71 kilometers.

怒江-尼罗河 输水发电管及超级高铁 | NuJiang - Nile PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国、缅甸、泰国、孟加拉、印度、巴基斯坦、伊朗、阿联酋、阿曼、也门、吉布提、沙特和埃及等，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of China, Myanmar, Thailand, Bangladesh, India, Pakistan, Iran, UAE, Oman, Yemen, Djibouti, Saudi Arabia and Egypt etc, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术。

输水发电管线将始于怒江中国云南段，终点位于尼罗河埃及段，含南亚-尼罗河段和怒江段，全长约13960公里；其中，南亚-尼罗河段沿南亚大陆板块海岸线向西，长约12635公里。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc.

The hydroelectric generation pipeline will start from the NuJiang River in YunNan of China and end at the Nile River in Egypt, including the South Asia-Nile Section and the NuJiang River Section, with total length of about 13960 km; the South Asia-Nile Section runs westward along the coastline of the South Asia continental plate, with length of about 12635 km.

苏伊士铁路运河 | Suez Railway Canal

2021-11-28
Projects

埃及，基于并行轨道的铁路运河项目

of Egypt, Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用基于并行轨道的铁路运河系统等专利技术。

轨道线路沿苏伊士运河西奈河岸，以取得较为平坦的道路，减少施工量，个别地点地势起伏略高，平均海拔高度8米，全长约155公里。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks System etc.

The tracks line will along the Sinai bank of Suez Canal to obtain a relatively flat road and reduce the construction works. The terrain of individual sites is slightly higher, with average altitude of 8 meters and total length of about 155 km.

亚东-古里格拉姆 输水发电管及超级高铁 | YaDong - Kurigram PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国、不丹、印度、孟加拉，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of China, Bhutan, India and Bangladesh, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术。

超级高铁起点拟于中国西藏亚东县，途径不丹、印度，终点在孟加拉古里格拉姆县境内，输水发电管全长约244公里。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc.

The Hyperloop will start from Yadong County of Tibet China, pass through Bhutan and India, and end in Kurigram District of Bangladesh. The total length of water transmission and hydroelectric generation pipeline is about 244km.
