苏伊士铁路运河 | Suez Railway Canal

2021-11-28
Projects

埃及，基于并行轨道的铁路运河项目

of Egypt, Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用基于并行轨道的铁路运河系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

轨道线路沿苏伊士运河西奈河岸，以取得较为平坦的道路，减少施工量，个别地点地势起伏略高，平均海拔高度8米，全长约155公里。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

The tracks line will along the Sinai bank of Suez Canal to obtain a relatively flat road and reduce the construction works. The terrain of individual sites is slightly higher, with average altitude of 8 meters and total length of about 155 km.

泰国-马来西亚铁路运河 | Thailand - Malaysia Railway Canal

2021-11-28
Projects

泰国、马来西亚，基于并行轨道的铁路运河项目

of Thailand and Malaysia, Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用基于并行轨道的铁路运河系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

轨道线路从泰国宋卡以南空旷海岸，避开机场等区域，向西南陆地直达对岸的马来西亚玻璃市以南空旷海岸，以取得较为平坦的道路，减少施工量，个别地点地势起伏略高，平均海拔高度49米，全长约100公里。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

The track line will start from the spare coast south of Thailand Songkhla, keep away from the airport and other areas, and go to the southwest land to the spare coast south of Malaysia Negeri Perlis City, so as to obtain a relatively flat road and reduce the construction works. The terrain of individual sites fluctuates slightly, with average altitude of 49 meters and total length of about 100 km.

亚东-古里格拉姆 输水发电管及超级高铁 | YaDong - Kurigram PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国、不丹、印度、孟加拉，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of China, Bhutan, India and Bangladesh, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

超级高铁起点拟于中国西藏亚东县，途径不丹、印度，终点在孟加拉古里格拉姆县境内，输水发电管全长约244公里。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

The Hyperloop will start from Yadong County of Tibet China, pass through Bhutan and India, and end in Kurigram District of Bangladesh. The total length of water transmission and hydroelectric generation pipeline is about 244km.

恩施-宜昌 输水发电管及超级高铁 | EnShi - YiChang PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国湖北，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Hubei China, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目是葛洲坝-清江 输水发电管及超级高铁的姊妹项目，采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

起点拟位于湖北恩施市，终点在湖北宜昌市枝江，沿清江顺流而下，全长约255公里，在宜万铁路恩施段的南部。

This project is the sister project of GeZhouBa - QingJiang PHG & NPACH, utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

It will start from EnShi of HuBei Province and end at ZhiJiang District of Yichang City, HuBei Province. It flows down the QingJiang River, with total length of about 255 km, at the south of EnShi section of YiChang-WanZhou Railway.
