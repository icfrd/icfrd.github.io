Projects

中国湖北，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Hubei China, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目是葛洲坝-清江 输水发电管及超级高铁的姊妹项目，采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术。

起点拟位于湖北恩施市，终点在湖北宜昌市枝江，沿清江顺流而下，全长约255公里，在宜万铁路恩施段的南部。

This project is the sister project of GeZhouBa - QingJiang PHG & NPACH, utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc.

It will start from EnShi of HuBei Province and end at ZhiJiang District of Yichang City, HuBei Province. It flows down the QingJiang River, with total length of about 255 km, at the south of EnShi section of YiChang-WanZhou Railway.

马兰奇-皮姆讷格尔 输水发电管及超级高铁 | Melamchi - Bhimnagar PHG & NPACH

Projects

尼泊尔马兰奇-印度皮姆讷格尔，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

Melamchi of Nepal - Bhimnagar of India, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目是马兰奇-加德满都 输水发电管及超级高铁的姊妹项目，采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术。

管线拟自尼泊尔马兰奇下游始，沿印德拉瓦提河顺流而下，流经桑科西河、都得科西河、阿龙河、科西河、直至印度比哈尔邦皮姆讷格尔境内，全长约294公里。

This project is the sister project of Melamchi - Kathmandu PHG & NPACH, utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc.

The pipeline will start from the lower reaches of Melamchi of Nepal, flows down Indrawati River, flows through Sunkoshi River, Dudhkosi River, Arun River and Kosi River, then reach Bhimnagar of Bihar India, with total length of about 294km.

温哥华-诺福克 输水发电管及超级高铁 | Vancouver - Norfolk PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

加拿大、美国，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Canada and USA, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术。

负压气垫超级高铁起点拟于加拿大温哥华市中心东端港口，沿现有公路进入美国境内，翻过落基山脉后，沿密苏里河流向东南方向，途径密歇根湖和伊利湖附近，以及华盛顿哥伦比亚特区，最终到达弗吉尼亚诺福克国际港口，超级高铁全长约5324公里（ 参考路线图：https://goo.gl/maps/WSBgNT8nQdbXGmiA6 ）。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc.

The Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop will start from the port at the Downtown Eastside of Vancouver Canada, enter the United States along the existing highway, go southeast along the Missouri River after crossing the Rocky Mountains, and pass nearby regions of the Lake Michigan and Lake Erie along the Missouri River, and Washington, D.C., then finally reach the Norfolk International Port of Virginia. The total length of the Hyperloop is about 5324 kilometers ( Roadmap for reference: https://goo.gl/maps/WSBgNT8nQdbXGmiA6 ).
