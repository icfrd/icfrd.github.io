2021-11-28
Projects

尼加拉瓜，基于并行轨道的铁路运河项目

of Nicaragua, Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用基于并行轨道的铁路运河系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

轨道线路从尼加拉瓜太平洋海岸，经过尼加拉瓜湖，到尼加拉瓜加勒比海岸，以取得较为平坦的道路，减少施工量，个别地点地势起伏略高，全长约160公里。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Railway Canal System by Parallel Tracks System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

The tracks line will run from the Pacific coast of Nicaragua, through the Lago Nicaraguan Lake to the Caribbean coast of Nicaragua, to obtain a relatively flat road and reduce the construction works. The terrain of individual sites is slightly higher, with total length of about 160 km.

莱茵河-泰晤士河 输水发电管及超级高铁 | Rhine - Thames PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

德国、瑞士、荷兰、比利时、法国和英国，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of the Germany, Switzerland, Netherlands, Belgium, France and the United Kingdom, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

输水发电管线将始于莱茵河中上游，海拔高度397米；途径德国、瑞士、荷兰、比利时、法国，横跨多佛尔海峡；终点位于英国泰晤士河中上游，海拔高度 22米；全长1255公里以上。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

The hydroelectric generation pipeline will start from the middle and upper reaches of the Rhine River, with altitude of 397 meters; It passes through Germany, Switzerland, the Netherlands, Belgium and France and crosses the Strait of Dover; The terminal is located in the middle and upper reaches of the Thames River in Britain, with altitude of 22 meters; The total length is more than 1255 kilometers.

温哥华-诺福克 输水发电管及超级高铁 | Vancouver - Norfolk PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

加拿大、美国，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Canada and USA, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统和基于风洞的助力系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

负压气垫超级高铁起点拟于加拿大温哥华市中心东端港口，沿现有公路进入美国境内，翻过落基山脉后，沿密苏里河流向东南方向，途径密歇根湖和伊利湖附近，以及华盛顿哥伦比亚特区，最终到达弗吉尼亚诺福克国际港口，超级高铁全长约5324公里（ 参考路线图：https://goo.gl/maps/WSBgNT8nQdbXGmiA6 ）。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System and Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

The Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop will start from the port at the Downtown Eastside of Vancouver Canada, enter the United States along the existing highway, go southeast along the Missouri River after crossing the Rocky Mountains, and pass nearby regions of the Lake Michigan and Lake Erie along the Missouri River, and Washington, D.C., then finally reach the Norfolk International Port of Virginia. The total length of the Hyperloop is about 5324 kilometers ( Roadmap for reference: https://goo.gl/maps/WSBgNT8nQdbXGmiA6 ).

Projects

东北亚，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

Northeast Asia, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

西线方案

West Line Plan

输水发电管线拟始于日本佐贺县关木水库，通过新修的对马海峡跨海大桥到韩国，沿朝鲜半岛西侧海岸线北上，经过朝鲜再沿海岸线向西，终点拟位于中国辽宁大连市东风水库，全长约1877公里以上，含西线主线和鸭绿江支线，其中西线主线长约1609公里以上；

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from the Sekigi Reservoir in Saga-ken of Japan, to South Korea through the newly built cross sea bridge over the Tsushima Strait, go north along the West coastline of the Korean Peninsula, pass through North Korea, and then go west along the coastline. The terminal point is planned to be located in DongFeng Reservoir in Dalian of LiaoNing China, with total length of more than 1877 km, including the Main Line and the YaLu River Branch Line of the West Line, of which the Main Line is more than 1609 km long;

中国福建、台湾，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁北线项目

FuJian and TaiWan of China, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop North Line Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

南路方案

South Road Plan

输水发电管线拟始于福建闽江上游水口水库，沿着闽江顺流而下，通过平潭-新竹新修的跨海大桥到台湾，终点拟位于台湾屏东龙銮潭，全长约680公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from the ShuiKou Reservoir in the upper reaches of the MinJiang River in FuJian of China, flow down the MinJiang River, and reach TaiWan of China through the newly built sea crossing bridge between PingTan and Hsinchu. The terminal point is planned to be located in LongLuanTan Reservoir in TaiWan Pingtung , with total length of more than 680km.
