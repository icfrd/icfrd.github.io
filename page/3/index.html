红河-台湾 输水发电管及超级高铁南线 | HongHe - TaiWan PPGS & NPACH South Line

2021-11-28
Projects

中国云南，越南，中国广西、广东、福建、台湾，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁南线项目

YunNan of China, Vietnam and GuangXi, GuangDong, FuJian, TaiWan of China, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop South Line Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Power Generation System System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

输水发电管线拟始于中国云南红河州马堵山水库，沿着红河顺流而下，途径越南到达红河出海口，沿海岸线向东，途径澳门和香港，至厦门到金门，通过新修的金门-澎湖-嘉义跨海大桥到中国台湾，终点拟与福建-台湾 输水发电管及超级高铁北线相连，全长约2192公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from MaDuShan Reservoir in HongHe Prefecture of YunNan China, and will flow down the HongHe river. It will pass through Vietnam to the estuary of the HongHe river, and will be along the coastline to the East, then pass through Macao and Hongkong to XiaMen and Kinmen, and then to the TaiWan of China through the newly built Kinmen - Penghu - Chiayi sea crossing bridge, the end point will be connected with the FuJian - TaiWan PPGS & NPACH North Line. The total length is about 2192 kilometers.

of the Switzerland, Germany, Austria, Slovakia, Hungary, Croatia, Serbia, Bulgaria, Romania, Moldova and Ukraine, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline will start from the middle and upper reaches of the Danube River, be connected with the Rhine - Thames PPGS & NPACH, pass through Germany, Austria, Slovakia, Hungary, Croatia, Serbia, Bulgaria, Romania, Moldova and Ukraine, and end at the Black Sea, with total length of more than 2413 kilometers.

of Italy, Albania, Greece, Turkey, Syria, Palestine, Israel and Egypt Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project
