人性化无延迟涡轮增压发动机 | Humanized Turbocharged Engine Without Delay

2021-11-17
Technology

专利名称 / PATENT NAME: 人性化无延迟涡轮增压发动机 / HUMANIZED TURBOCHARGED ENGINE WITHOUT DELAY

简介 / BRIEF

对曲轴箱的压力集中收集，并保持压力在设定值；不使用外置设备，彻底解决涡轮增压发动机的延迟缺陷。

Collect the pressure of crankcase intensively and keep the pressure at the set value. No external equipment is used to completely solve the delay defect of turbocharged engine.

  • 抬起油门时，切断发动机给涡轮增压的供气，避免陷入涡轮增压2秒延迟的死亡陷阱！

While lifting the throttle, cut off the air supply from the engine to the turbocharger to avoid falling into the death trap of 2-second delay by turbocharger!

  • 踩下油门，在发动机转速不足以推动涡轮介入时，释放集中收集的压力，推动涡轮转动！

While the accelerator is pressed, if the engine speed is not enough to push the turbine to work, release the collected pressure from crankcase and push the turbine to rotate!

货币价值锚定兑换机制 | Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value

2021-11-16
Technology

专利名称 / PATENT NAME: 货币价值锚定兑换机制 / EXCHANGE MECHANISM BY ANCHORED CURRENCY VALUE

简介 / BRIEF

标准货币的基础价值与普通人日常所饮用纯净水的水量锚定，如果有人连口水都喝不上，你就能知道世界哪里发生了什么不合理的事情。

货币质量和总量锚定可肉眼观测的海水水质和海平面高度，用户得以自主判断和决定单枚货币的品质及其价值。

The basic value of standard currency is anchored to the amount of purified water that ordinary people drink every day. If someone can’t even drink a mouthful of water, you can know what unreasonable things have happened and where the point is in the world.

The quality and total amount of currency are anchored to the visually observable seawater quality and sea level height, so that you can independently judge and determine the quality and value of a single coin.
