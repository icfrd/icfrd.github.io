Projects

德国、瑞士、荷兰、比利时、法国和英国，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of the Germany, Switzerland, Netherlands, Belgium, France and the United Kingdom, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

输水发电管线将始于莱茵河中上游，海拔高度397米；途径德国、瑞士、荷兰、比利时、法国，横跨多佛尔海峡；终点位于英国泰晤士河中上游，海拔高度 22米；全长1255公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline will start from the middle and upper reaches of the Rhine River, with altitude of 397 meters; It passes through Germany, Switzerland, the Netherlands, Belgium and France and crosses the Strait of Dover; The terminal is located in the middle and upper reaches of the Thames River in Britain, with altitude of 22 meters; The total length is more than 1255 kilometers.

怒江-尼罗河 输水发电管及超级高铁 | NuJiang - Nile PPGS & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国、缅甸、泰国、孟加拉、印度、巴基斯坦、伊朗、阿联酋、阿曼、也门、吉布提、沙特和埃及等，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of China, Myanmar, Thailand, Bangladesh, India, Pakistan, Iran, UAE, Oman, Yemen, Djibouti, Saudi Arabia and Egypt etc, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

主线 | Main Line

输水发电管线将始于怒江中国云南段，终点位于尼罗河埃及段，含南亚-尼罗河段、怒江段和中非地区支线，全长约14188公里；其中，南亚-尼罗河段沿南亚大陆板块海岸线向西，长约12635公里。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline will start from the NuJiang River in YunNan of China and end at the Nile River in Egypt, including the South Asia-Nile Section, the NuJiang River Section and the Djibouti Branch Line, with total length of about 14188 km; the South Asia-Nile Section runs westward along the coastline of the South Asia continental plate, with length of about 12635 km.

拉古纳萨拉达-温哥华-诺福克 输水发电管及超级高铁 | Laguna Salada - Vancouver - Norfolk PPGS & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

加拿大、美国、墨西哥，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Canada, USA and Mexico, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

温哥华 - 诺福克 | Vancouver - Norfolk

温哥华 - 诺福克负压气垫超级高铁起点拟于加拿大温哥华市中心东端港口，沿现有公路进入美国境内，翻过落基山脉后，沿密苏里河流向东南方向，途径密歇根湖和伊利湖附近，以及华盛顿哥伦比亚特区，最终到达弗吉尼亚诺福克国际港口，超级高铁全长约5324公里（ 参考路线图：https://goo.gl/maps/WSBgNT8nQdbXGmiA6 ）。

The Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop of Vancouver - Norfolk will start from the port at the Downtown Eastside of Vancouver Canada, enter the United States along the existing highway, go southeast along the Missouri River after crossing the Rocky Mountains, and pass nearby regions of the Lake Michigan and Lake Erie along the Missouri River, and Washington, D.C., then finally reach the Norfolk International Port of Virginia. The total length of the Hyperloop is about 5324 kilometers ( Roadmap for reference: https://goo.gl/maps/WSBgNT8nQdbXGmiA6 ).

Projects

环加勒比，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

Circum Caribbean, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

负压气垫超级高铁系统全长约14638公里。

The total length of Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop is about 14638 km.

说明：本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

Footnote: This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Power Generation System System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.
