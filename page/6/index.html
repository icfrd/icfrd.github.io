怒江-尼罗河 输水发电管及超级高铁 | NuJiang - Nile PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国、缅甸、泰国、孟加拉、印度、巴基斯坦、伊朗、阿联酋、阿曼、也门、吉布提、沙特和埃及等，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of China, Myanmar, Thailand, Bangladesh, India, Pakistan, Iran, UAE, Oman, Yemen, Djibouti, Saudi Arabia and Egypt etc, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

输水发电管线将始于怒江中国云南段，终点位于尼罗河埃及段，含南亚-尼罗河段、怒江段和中非地区支线，全长约14188公里；其中，南亚-尼罗河段沿南亚大陆板块海岸线向西，长约12635公里。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline will start from the NuJiang River in YunNan of China and end at the Nile River in Egypt, including the South Asia-Nile Section, the NuJiang River Section and the Djibouti Branch Line, with total length of about 14188 km; the South Asia-Nile Section runs westward along the coastline of the South Asia continental plate, with length of about 12635 km.

负压气垫超级高铁系统 | Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System

2021-11-21
Technology

专利名称 / PATENT NAME: 负压气垫超级高铁系统 / NEGATIVE PRESSURE AIR CUSHION HYPERLOOP SYSTEM

简介 / BRIEF

无需真空、无需磁悬浮、不耗电；通过喷射高压气体，在车前形成负压空间，车身四周形成气垫层，车后给予正压推力；可同时输送（增值）气体，具有施工成本低、结构简单、安全可靠、运行成本低、启停快，能转弯、上下坡等特点！

No need vacuum, no magnetic levitation, no electricity consumption; By spraying high-pressure gas, negative pressure space is formed in front of the vehicle, and air cushion layer is formed around the vehicle body, and positive pressure thrust is given behind the vehicle; It can transport (value-added) gas at the same time. It has the characteristics of low construction cost, simple structure, safety and reliability, low operation cost, fast start and stop, can turning, uphill and downhill, etc!
