第聂伯河-卡拉博加兹湾 输水发电管及超级高铁 | Dnieper - Garabogazköl PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

乌克兰、俄罗斯、格鲁吉亚、阿塞拜疆、伊朗、土库曼斯坦，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Ukraine, Russia, Georgia, Azerbaijan, Iran and Turkmenistan Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

输水发电管线将始于第聂伯河出海口，途径乌克兰、俄罗斯、格鲁吉亚、阿塞拜疆、伊朗和土库曼斯坦，终点拟位于卡拉博加兹湾内，长2974公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline of Dnieper - Black Sea Line will start from the estuary of Dnieper River, pass through Ukraine, Russia, Georgia, Azerbaijan, Iran and Turkmenistan, and end in Garabogazköl Aylagy, with length of more than 2974 km.

福建-台湾 输水发电管及超级高铁北线 | FuJian - TaiWan PHG & NPACH North Line

2021-11-28
Projects

中国福建、台湾，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁北线项目

FuJian and TaiWan of China, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop North Line Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

南路方案 | South Road Plan

输水发电管线拟始于福建闽江上游水口水库，沿着闽江顺流而下，通过平潭-新竹新修的跨海大桥到台湾，终点拟位于台湾屏东龙銮潭，全长约680公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from the ShuiKou Reservoir in the upper reaches of the MinJiang River in FuJian of China, flow down the MinJiang River, and reach TaiWan of China through the newly built sea crossing bridge between PingTan and Hsinchu. The terminal point is planned to be located in LongLuanTan Reservoir in TaiWan Pingtung , with total length of more than 680km.

东北亚西线，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

West Line of Northeast Asia, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

输水发电管线拟始于日本佐贺县关木水库，通过新修的对马海峡跨海大桥到韩国，沿朝鲜半岛西侧海岸线北上，经过朝鲜再沿海岸线向西，终点拟位于中国辽宁大连市东风水库，全长约1877公里以上，含西线主线和鸭绿江支线，其中西线主线长约1609公里以上；

The terminal point is planned to be located in DongFeng Reservoir in Dalian of LiaoNing China, with total length of more than 1877 km, including the Main Line and the YaLu River Branch Line of the West Line, of which the Main Line is more than 1609 km long;

澜沧江-喀尔克 输水发电管及超级高铁 | LanCang River - Kirke River PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国、缅甸、老挝、泰国、柬埔寨、越南、马来西亚、新加坡、印度尼西亚、巴布新几内亚、澳大利亚，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Myanmar, Laos, Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, Papua New Guinea and Australia Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

输水发电管线将始于澜沧江下游，途径缅甸、老挝、泰国、柬埔寨，到达湄公河越南出海口，沿海岸线经泰国、马来西亚、新加坡、印度尼西亚、巴布新几内亚，其中将修建桥港一体的跨海大桥和隧道，最终到达澳大利亚喀尔克河出海口内湖，全长11186公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline will start from the lower reaches of LanCang River, pass through Myanmar, Laos, Thailand and Cambodia, reach the Sea outlet of Mekong River in Vietnam, then pass through Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia and Papua New Guinea along the coastline, in whichbridge and port integrated sea crossing tunnels will be built, and finally reach the inner lake at the Sea outlet of Kirke River in Australia, the total length is more than 11186 kilometers.
