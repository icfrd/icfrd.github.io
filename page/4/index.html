of Ukraine, Russia, Georgia, Azerbaijan, Iran and Turkmenistan Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

主线 | Main Line

输水发电管线将始于第聂伯河出海口，途径乌克兰、俄罗斯、格鲁吉亚、阿塞拜疆、伊朗和土库曼斯坦，终点拟位于卡拉博加兹湾内，长2974公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline of Dnieper - Black Sea Line will start from the estuary of Dnieper River, pass through Ukraine, Russia, Georgia, Azerbaijan, Iran and Turkmenistan, and end in Garabogazköl Aylagy, with length of more than 2974 km.

含图们江线和珲春支线，全长约300公里以上；

Including Tumen River Line and HunChun Branch Line, with total length of more than 300km;

图们江线 | Tumen River Line

图们江线输水发电管线拟始于中国吉林延边海兰水库，到达图们江顺流而下，直至朝鲜罗先市最东端的港口，长约222公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from HaiLan Reservoir in YanBian of JiLin China, flow down the Tumen River and reach the easternmost port of Raseon, North Korea, with total length of more than 222 km;

福建-台湾 输水发电管及超级高铁北线 | FuJian - TaiWan PPGS & NPACH North Line

2021-11-28
Projects

中国福建、台湾，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁北线项目

FuJian and TaiWan of China, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop North Line Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

南路方案 | South Road Plan

输水发电管线拟始于福建闽江上游水口水库，沿着闽江顺流而下，通过平潭-新竹新修的跨海大桥到台湾，终点拟位于台湾屏东龙銮潭，全长约680公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from the ShuiKou Reservoir in the upper reaches of the MinJiang River in FuJian of China, flow down the MinJiang River, and reach TaiWan of China through the newly built sea crossing bridge between PingTan and Hsinchu. The terminal point is planned to be located in LongLuanTan Reservoir in TaiWan Pingtung , with total length of more than 680km.

东北亚西线 输水发电管及超级高铁 | Northeast Asia West Line PPGS & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

东北亚西线，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

West Line of Northeast Asia, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

输水发电管线拟始于日本佐贺县关木水库，通过新修的对马海峡跨海大桥到韩国，沿朝鲜半岛西侧海岸线北上，经过朝鲜再沿海岸线向西，终点拟位于中国辽宁大连市东风水库，全长约1877公里以上，含西线主线和鸭绿江支线，其中西线主线长约1609公里以上；

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from the Sekigi Reservoir in Saga-ken of Japan, to South Korea through the newly built cross sea bridge over the Tsushima Strait, go north along the West coastline of the Korean Peninsula, pass through North Korea, and then go west along the coastline. The terminal point is planned to be located in DongFeng Reservoir in Dalian of LiaoNing China, with total length of more than 1877 km, including the Main Line and the YaLu River Branch Line of the West Line, of which the Main Line is more than 1609 km long;
