澜沧江-喀尔克 输水发电管及超级高铁 | LanCang River - Kirke River PPGS & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国、缅甸、老挝、泰国、柬埔寨、越南、马来西亚、新加坡、印度尼西亚、巴布新几内亚、澳大利亚，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Myanmar, Laos, Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, Papua New Guinea and Australia Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

主线 | Main Line

输水发电管线将始于澜沧江下游，途径缅甸、老挝、泰国、柬埔寨，到达湄公河越南出海口，沿海岸线经泰国、马来西亚、新加坡、印度尼西亚、巴布新几内亚，其中将修建桥港一体的跨海大桥和隧道，最终到达澳大利亚喀尔克河出海口内湖，全长11186公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline will start from the lower reaches of LanCang River, pass through Myanmar, Laos, Thailand and Cambodia, reach the Sea outlet of Mekong River in Vietnam, then pass through Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia and Papua New Guinea along the coastline, in whichbridge and port integrated sea crossing tunnels will be built, and finally reach the inner lake at the Sea outlet of Kirke River in Australia, the total length is more than 11186 kilometers.

2021-11-28
Projects

尼泊尔马兰奇-印度皮姆讷格尔，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

Melamchi of Nepal - Bhimnagar of India, Pipeline Power Generation System & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

管线拟自尼泊尔马兰奇下游始，沿印德拉瓦提河顺流而下，流经桑科西河、都得科西河、阿龙河、科西河、直至印度比哈尔邦皮姆讷格尔境内，全长约294公里。

The pipeline will start from the lower reaches of Melamchi of Nepal, flows down Indrawati River, flows through Sunkoshi River, Dudhkosi River, Arun River and Kosi River, then reach Bhimnagar of Bihar India, with total length of about 294km.
