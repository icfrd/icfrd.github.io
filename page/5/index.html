红河-台湾 输水发电管及超级高铁南线 | HongHe - TaiWan PHG & NPACH South Line

2021-11-28
Projects

中国云南，越南，中国广西、广东、福建、台湾，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁南线项目

YunNan of China, Vietnam and GuangXi, GuangDong, FuJian, TaiWan of China, Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop South Line Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

输水发电管线拟始于中国云南红河州马堵山水库，沿着红河顺流而下，途径越南到达红河出海口，沿海岸线向东，途径澳门和香港，至厦门到金门，通过新修的金门-澎湖-嘉义跨海大桥到中国台湾，终点拟与福建-台湾 输水发电管及超级高铁北线相连，全长约2192公里以上。

The hydroelectric generation pipeline is planned to start from MaDuShan Reservoir in HongHe Prefecture of YunNan China, and will flow down the HongHe river. It will pass through Vietnam to the estuary of the HongHe river, and will be along the coastline to the East, then pass through Macao and Hongkong to XiaMen and Kinmen, and then to the TaiWan of China through the newly built Kinmen - Penghu - Chiayi sea crossing bridge, the end point will be connected with the FuJian - TaiWan PHG & NPACH North Line. The total length is about 2192 kilometers.

澜沧江-喀尔克 输水发电管及超级高铁 | LanCang River - Kirke River PHG & NPACH

2021-11-28
Projects

中国、缅甸、老挝、泰国、柬埔寨、越南、马来西亚、新加坡、印度尼西亚、巴布新几内亚、澳大利亚，输水发电管及负压气垫超级高铁项目

of Myanmar, Laos, Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, Papua New Guinea and Australia Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation & Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop Project

项目概述 / PROJECT OVERVIEW

本项目采用输送管发电系统、负压气垫超级高铁系统、基于风洞的助力系统，以及桥港一体化系统等专利技术，并且兼容经纬国际网路由通信协议、符合货币价值锚定兑换机制。

输水发电管线将始于澜沧江下游，途径缅甸、老挝、泰国、柬埔寨，到达湄公河越南出海口，沿海岸线经泰国、马来西亚、新加坡、印度尼西亚、巴布新几内亚，其中将修建桥港一体的跨海大桥和隧道，最终到达澳大利亚喀尔克河出海口内湖，全长11186公里以上。

This project utilizes the patented technologies of Pipeline Hydroelectric Generation System, Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System, Wind Tunnel Based Power Accelerated System and Bridge & Port for Container Integrated System etc, and compatible with the routing and communication protocol of Longitude & Latitude Based Internat, conforms to the Exchange Mechanism by Anchored Currency Value.

The hydroelectric generation pipeline will start from the lower reaches of LanCang River, pass through Myanmar, Laos, Thailand and Cambodia, reach the Sea outlet of Mekong River in Vietnam, then pass through Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia and Papua New Guinea along the coastline, in whichbridge and port integrated sea crossing tunnels will be built, and finally reach the inner lake at the Sea outlet of Kirke River in Australia, the total length is more than 11186 kilometers.

负压气垫超级高铁系统 | Negative Pressure Air Cushion Hyperloop System

2021-11-21
Technology

专利名称 / PATENT NAME: 负压气垫超级高铁系统 / NEGATIVE PRESSURE AIR CUSHION HYPERLOOP SYSTEM

简介 / BRIEF

无需真空、无需磁悬浮、不耗电；通过喷射高压气体，在车前形成负压空间，车身四周形成气垫层，车后给予正压推力；可同时输送（增值）气体，具有施工成本低、结构简单、安全可靠、运行成本低、启停快，能转弯、上下坡等特点！

No need vacuum, no magnetic levitation, no electricity consumption; By spraying high-pressure gas, negative pressure space is formed in front of the vehicle, and air cushion layer is formed around the vehicle body, and positive pressure thrust is given behind the vehicle; It can transport (value-added) gas at the same time. It has the characteristics of low construction cost, simple structure, safety and reliability, low operation cost, fast start and stop, can turning, uphill and downhill, etc!
