高海拔水电技术委员会

是 国际面板坝协会 致力于高海拔水电和技术应用的专业委员会。

High-Altitude Hydroelectricity & Technology Committee is the special committee of the International CFRD Society, who dedicated to high altitude hydroelectricity and technology applications .
