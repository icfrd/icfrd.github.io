资金澄清 | CAPITAL CLARIFICATION

投资或合作前应明确资金来源，资金必须通过合法手段获得，不得通过欺诈、色情、毒品、掠夺、伤害、战争和/或其他非法手段获得；不能以合法的名义通过贪婪和/或阻碍社会进步而获得；否则将不会被接受或作为无效而被返回，除非得到普遍原谅。

THE SOURCE OF FUNDS SHOULD BE CLARIFIED BEFORE INVESTED OR COOPERATED, IT MUST BE OBTAINED THROUGH LEGAL MEANS, NOT THROUGH FRAUD, PORN, DRUGS, PLUNDER, WOUND, WAR AND / OR OTHER ILLEGAL MEANS; IT CAN NOT BE OBTAINED IN THE NAME OF LEGALITY BY MEANS OF GREED AND / OR HINDERING SOCIAL PROGRESS; OTHERWISE IT WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED OR RETURNED AS INVALID, UNLESS BE UNIVERSALLY FORGIVEN.

心存顾虑的投资人在对项目清单中的贫困地区进行无偿援助后，或将经澄清后的产业投入到历史欠账地区后，将相应地免于澄清规则限制，但仍受普遍原谅规则的约束。

THE INVESTOR’S MISGIVINGS WILL BE EXEMPTED FROM THE CLARIFICATION RULE EQUIVALENTLY, AFTER PROVIDING FREE ASSISTANCE TO THE POOR AREAS IN THE PROJECT LIST OR PUTTING THE CLARIFIED INDUSTRY INTO THE AREAS WITH HISTORICAL DEBTS, BUT WILL STILL BE SUBJECTED TO THE UNIVERSAL FORGIVENESS RULE.
