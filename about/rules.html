Capital Clarification Rule

THE SOURCE OF FUNDS SHOULD BE CLARIFIED BEFORE INVESTED OR COOPERATED, IT MUST BE OBTAINED THROUGH LEGAL MEANS, NOT THROUGH FRAUD, PORN, DRUGS, PLUNDER, WAR AND / OR OTHER ILLEGAL MEANS; OTHERWISE IT WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED OR RETURNED AS INVALID, UNLESS BE UNIVERSALLY FORGIVEN.
